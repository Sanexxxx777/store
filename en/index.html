★ process and terms

How the work runs

Four steps, no surprises. Below are answers to what people usually ask before reaching out.

  1. We discuss the job. Telegram or email, you briefly describe what you need.
  2. We fix the scope. Input, output and acceptance check in writing, before any code. At this point I quote the timeline and exact price.
  3. The sprint. Usually 2-4 days. A progress check-in if the work runs longer than two days.
  4. Acceptance and handover. We check the result against the fixed criterion, and I hand over the code and setup instructions.
What does "from $X" mean, what is the exact price?

"From" is the price of the sprint's minimum scope. The exact number is quoted once the scope is fixed at step 2, and it does not change after that. If the job grows, we revisit the scope first, not the invoice.

How does payment work?

Discussed in chat: card, crypto or transfer. Usually part upfront, the rest on acceptance. There are no payment buttons on the site yet, on purpose.

What if the result does not fit?

The acceptance check is fixed before the start; fit or no fit is checked against it, not against a feeling. If it falls short of the criterion, I finish the work within the sprint at no extra charge.

Who owns the code?

You do. I hand over the source and usage rights; Living Mascot ships under the license included in the package.
